# Market Analysis Tableau Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview
A comprehensive Market Analysis dashboard built using Tableau to track overall business performance, profitability trends, and product-level metrics. This project provides a clear, high-level view of key financial indicators, seasonal profitability, category performance, and geographical sales distribution across the United States.


## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

 **Data Visualization & Analysis:** Tableau.

## 📊 Core KPIs

1- **Total Sales:** $2,297,201

2- **Total Profit:** $286,397

3- **Overall Profit Margin:** 12%

4- **Total Quantity Sold:** 37,873

## 💡 Key Business Insights

1- **Seasonal Profitability:** Quarter 4 (Q4) is overwhelmingly the most profitable period of the year, generating $110,622 in profit, which is more than double the profit of Q1 ($48,024). The historical trend chart confirms consistent spikes in Q4 across multiple years (e.g., 2017 Q4 and 2018 Q4).

2- **Category Performance:** The "Technology" category is the primary profit driver, contributing $145,455. "Office Supplies" follows closely with $122,491, while "Furniture" is severely lagging, generating only $18,451 in profit.

3- **Top Performing Products:** The "Canon imageCLASS 2200 Advanced Copier" is the absolute top-performing product, generating a massive $25,199.93 in profit, outperforming the second-best product (Fellowes PB500 Electric Punch) by more than 3x.

4- **Geographical Sales Distribution:** The sales heatmap indicates that the West and South regions are strongholds. California is the dominant market leader, followed by Texas ($170,188) and Washington ($138,641).

5- **Underperforming Products:** Items like "Eureka Disposable Bags for Sanitaire" (1.624) and "Avery 5" (5.760) represent the bottom of the sales barrel, indicating stagnant inventory.


## 🚀 Strategic Recommendations


1- **Capitalize on Q4 Seasonality:** Since Q4 drives the vast majority of the company's profit, inventory and marketing budgets should be heavily optimized and front-loaded in Q3 to ensure maximum readiness for the end-of-year surge.

2- **Reevaluate the Furniture Category:** With "Furniture" contributing only a fraction of the total profit, a strategic review is necessary. The company should consider either adjusting pricing strategies, optimizing shipping costs, or reducing the catalog size for this category to protect the 12% profit margin.

3- **Targeted Regional Campaigns:** While California and Texas are performing exceptionally well, marketing efforts should be expanded to underperforming central states to balance the geographical revenue stream.

4- **Inventory Management:** Discontinue or bundle the bottom 5 products by sales to clear warehouse space and redirect capital toward high-margin Technology products like the Canon imageCLASS series.
